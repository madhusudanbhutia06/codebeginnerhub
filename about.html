About CodeBeginnerHub

CodeBeginnerHub is a beginner-friendly tech blog created to help students and newcomers understand coding and web development in simple English.

This blog focuses on explaining concepts like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern tech tools in a clear and practical way.

The goal of CodeBeginnerHub is to make learning technology easy, accessible, and useful for everyone.
